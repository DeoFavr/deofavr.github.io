About

Deo Favr (formerly Don Rodeo) is an Afrobeat and Afropop artist from Nigeria. With a degree in both music and social sciences, he creates powerful soundscapes that blend conscious themes with infectious rhythms. Influenced by Snoop Dogg, Notorious B.I.G., Juice WRLD, and Enya, his sound bridges the spiritual and the street.
